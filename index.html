About Lauren

I have been a classical musician my entire life. After graduating from music school, I performed, composed, and taught music for a living for 10 years. While I loved the work that I did, I felt that a career in music was not for me. Then I discoved coding and realized it was actually very similar to learning and composing music, but with much better career prospects :)

After teaching myself some basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript basics through free online resources, I decided I wanted a more structured learning process. I started looking into coding bootcamps and that's when I found Thinkful. I'm so thrilled to start my journey as a web developer, and I can't wait to work on projects and collaborate with other developers!
